Fiduciary Real Estate



Fiduciary is the highest legal duty of one party to another. We assist with our clients' needs with their best interests in mind.

Our fiduciary duty to our clients consist of six components: obedience, loyalty, disclosure, confidentiality, accounting, and reasonable care and diligence.
With this duty to our clients, we will:

  • Obtain the best price and terms possible
  • Investigate and explain all offers
  • Disclose our true opinion of the property's value
  • Discuss possible negotiating strategies or options available to our client
  • Seek motivation of the other party to buy or sell
  • Guide to arrive at a reasonable listing price
  • Affirmatively discover relevant facts and disclose them
  • Investigate the material facts related to the sale
  • Prepare and explain from over 200 real estate legal documents
  • Help with meeting deadlines and closing dates
  • ... and many more to assist our clients with fiduciary duty
